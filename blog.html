About Us

Our Story

Girlsin.tech was founded in 2023 with a powerful mission: to create a supportive platform where girls and women in technology can share their journeys, learnings, and experiences. What started as a small collective of passionate technologists has grown into a vibrant community empowering women across all stages of their tech careers.

Our Approach

We believe in the power of shared experiences and authentic storytelling. Our platform combines educational content with real-world project stories and career insights, creating a resource that's both practical and inspiring. We focus on making technology more accessible and inclusive while celebrating the unique perspectives women bring to the field.

Our Values

Community

We foster an inclusive space where women in tech can connect, collaborate, and support each other through challenges and celebrations.

Authenticity

We encourage sharing real experiences—both successes and setbacks—because honest stories inspire and prepare others for their own journeys.

Education

We believe in continuous learning and making technical knowledge accessible to women at all stages of their career development.

Empowerment

We're committed to equipping women with the tools, confidence, and support network they need to thrive in technology fields.
